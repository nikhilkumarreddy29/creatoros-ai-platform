Title: Brand Voice Guide

The creator's tone should be simple, practical, educational, friendly, and confident.
Avoid overpromising, fake urgency, hate speech, toxic language, and unsupported claims.
Use short paragraphs and clear examples.


Title: YouTube Script Framework

A strong YouTube script should include:
1. Hook
2. Problem
3. Simple explanation
4. Practical example
5. Actionable takeaway
6. Call to comment or subscribe


Title: LinkedIn Post Framework

A strong LinkedIn post should include:
1. Strong opening line
2. Short paragraphs
3. Real-world insight
4. Simple numbered points
5. Final question for engagement


Title: CreatorOS Strategy Rules

High-trend topics should be converted into:
1. YouTube video ideas
2. LinkedIn posts
3. Short-form hooks
4. Community questions
5. Newsletter ideas
